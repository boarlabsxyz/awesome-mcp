How to Connect

  1. Click "+ Add Tool"
  2. Choose your MCP type (e.g., Google Docs)
  3. Name your instance and connect with Google
  4. Press the "Copy URL" button
  5. In Claude or ChatGPT, add the URL as a custom MCP connector
  6. On first use, you'll be asked to log in with Google via Auth0
  7. After login, your tools are ready to use

Access Rules

  • Allowed Orgs — your org is always included. Check external orgs to see their shared channels and DMs.
  • Blacklist Users — search by name. Blocks their DMs and any group DM they're in.
  • Whitelist Patterns — glob patterns for channel names. Use * for all, eng-* for prefix match.
  • Blacklist Patterns — channels matching these are blocked even if whitelisted.
  • Public Only — hides all private channels.
